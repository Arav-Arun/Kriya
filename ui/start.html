Everything Kriya can handle

Kriya turns the full Hyperface Credit Stack into plain-language actions. Ask in your own words on any channel. Kriya checks the live account, verifies it's really you before anything risky, and gets it done.

Accounts & Balances

  • Account Summary: current balance, credit/cash limits, currency, card snapshot
  • Account Details: full record: product, billing cycle, status, dates
  • Update Credit Limit: increase or decrease the approved credit limit

Card Management

  • Lock Card: temporarily freeze the card (reversible)
  • Unlock Card: remove the temporary lock
  • Hotlist Card: permanently disable a lost or stolen card (irreversible)
  • Replace Card: order a replacement card (damaged/lost)
  • View Card Controls: see which channels (online, POS, contactless, ATM, international) are enabled
  • Set Card Controls: toggle channels and adjust per-channel limits

Transactions & Statements

  • Fetch Transactions: list recent transactions with merchant, amount, status
  • Billed Transactions: charges that appear on the last statement
  • Unbilled Transactions: charges in the current cycle not yet billed
  • Transaction Inquiry: look up a single transaction by id or reference
  • Fetch Statements: statement history with totals, dues, and dates
  • Download Statement: get the statement document (PDF)

EMI & Pay Later

  • EMI Config: check EMI eligibility: available tenures, rates, and monthly installment
  • Create EMI: convert a purchase to equated monthly installments
  • EMI List: all EMI plans (active, closed, foreclosed)
  • Foreclosure Details: exact charges to close an EMI early
  • Foreclose EMI: close an EMI plan early and settle the remaining balance

Rewards & Points

  • Rewards Summary: current points balance and value
  • Rewards Ledger: per-entry earn/redeem/expire log
  • Reward Account: earning rates, tier, multiplier configuration
  • Reward Transactions: full earn/burn history with date ranges
  • Expiring Rewards: points about to expire, with dates
  • Credit Reward Points: add points (goodwill, promo)
  • Debit Reward Points: redeem points as statement credit or voucher

Cashback

  • Cashback Summary: total cashback earned in a date range
  • Cashback Transactions: per-transaction cashback earned/pending/credited

Benefits & Perks

  • Fetch Benefits: benefits available to this customer/account
  • Fetch Benefits by Program: all benefits on the card program
  • Subscribe to Benefit: opt in to a benefit (lounge access, insurance, partner offer)
  • Unsubscribe from Benefit: opt out of a subscribed benefit

Disputes, Fees & Escalations

  • Raise Dispute/Chargeback: file a formal dispute on a settled transaction, tracked per RBI timelines
  • Track Dispute: check the status, provisional credit, and resolution timeline
  • Initiate Refund: credit back an eligible transaction immediately
  • Waive Fee: auto-waive a late/annual fee (max 1 per year, max ₹1,000, policy-gated)
  • Create Escalation: escalate to a human operations team with full AI investigation summary
  • Check Payment Status: verify whether a repayment was received and credited
  • Fraud Liability Assessment: RBI-compliant limited-liability check within 3 working days
