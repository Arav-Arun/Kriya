Everything Kriya can handle

Kriya turns the Hyperface Credit Stack into plain language agentic actions. Ask in your own words on any channel. It checks the live account, answers in plain language , verifies it's really you before anything risky, and gets it done.

Accounts & Balances

  • Account Summary: current balance, credit/cash limits, currency, card snapshot
  • Account Details: full record: product, billing cycle, status, dates
  • Update Credit Limit: increase or decrease the approved credit limit

Card Management

  • Lock Card: temporarily freeze the card (reversible)
  • Unlock Card: remove the temporary lock
  • Hotlist Card: permanently disable a lost or stolen card (irreversible)
  • Replace Card: order a replacement card (damaged/lost)
  • Usage Controls: toggle online, POS, contactless, ATM, and international usage instantly

Transactions & Statements

  • Fetch Transactions: list recent transactions over any date window
  • Billed Transactions: charges that appear on the last statement
  • Unbilled Transactions: charges in the current cycle, building toward your next bill
  • Transaction Inquiry: look up a single transaction by id or reference
  • Fetch Statements: statement history with totals, dues, and dates
  • Download Statement: get the statement document (PDF)

EMI & Pay Later

  • EMI Config: check EMI eligibility: available tenures, rates, and monthly installment
  • Create EMI: convert a purchase to equated monthly installments
  • EMI List: all EMI plans (active, closed, foreclosed)
  • Foreclosure Details: exact charges to close an EMI early
  • Foreclose EMI: close an EMI plan early and settle the remaining balance

Rewards & Cashback

  • Reward Points: live points balance, including earned, redeemed, expired, and what's available
  • Reward Ledger: every points posting and the transaction or benefit that earned it
  • Redeem Points: redeem available reward points against the account
  • Cashback Summary: cashback earned, accrued, and reversed on your card
  • Cashback Activity: cashback per transaction, and why a charge did or didn't earn it

Disputes, Fees & Escalations

  • Raise Dispute/Chargeback: file a formal dispute on a settled transaction, tracked per RBI timelines
  • Track Dispute: check the status, provisional credit, and resolution timeline
  • Initiate Refund: credit back an eligible transaction immediately
  • Waive Fee: auto-waive a late/annual fee (max 1 per year, max ₹1,00,000, policy-gated)
  • Create Escalation: escalate to a human operations team with full AI investigation summary
  • Fraud Liability Assessment: RBI-compliant limited-liability check within 3 working days

Insights & Experience

  • Spend Insights: where your money goes, with a category breakdown, biggest purchase, and the bill building this cycle
  • Visual Answers: balance, transactions and EMI plans rendered as clean cards right in the chat
  • Benefits & Perks: lounge access, insurance, and partner offers on your card
  • Voice, Your Language: ask in Hindi, Hinglish, or English and hear the answer back
  • Always Logged: every check and action is recorded for a complete audit trail
