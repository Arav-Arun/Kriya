# CASE-0016

- Category: Duplicate Charge
- Priority: Low
- Assigned Team: Disputes Operations
- Policy Reference: POL-001
- Resolution Time: 4 working days
- Resolved On: 2025-08-18

## Customer Complaint

Customer says the BigBasket payment page timed out, they retried, and both attempts of ₹34,592 were charged.

## Investigation Findings

Both transactions settled; gateway logs confirm a timeout-and-retry sequence. Merchant order system shows a single fulfilled order.

## Resolution

Merchant confirmed duplicate processing; duplicate amount of ₹34,592 reversed to the card. Customer notified and case closed as Duplicate Reversed.
