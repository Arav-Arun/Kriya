# CASE-0045

- Category: Duplicate Charge
- Priority: Medium
- Assigned Team: Disputes Operations
- Policy Reference: POL-001
- Resolution Time: 7 working days
- Resolved On: 2025-10-17

## Customer Complaint

Customer disputes a second ₹69,265 charge from Zomato; merchant app shows only one confirmed order.

## Investigation Findings

Authorization logs show two settled transactions at Zomato for ₹69,265 within 11 minutes with identical terminal and order metadata. No recurring-billing pattern on the account.

## Resolution

Merchant confirmed duplicate processing; duplicate amount of ₹69,265 reversed to the card. Customer notified and case closed as Duplicate Reversed.
