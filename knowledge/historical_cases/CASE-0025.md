# CASE-0025

- Category: EMI Conversion
- Priority: Low
- Assigned Team: Customer Service
- Policy Reference: POL-004
- Resolution Time: 2 working days
- Resolved On: 2025-09-05

## Customer Complaint

Customer wants to convert a Ola purchase of ₹38,056 into 12-month EMI.

## Investigation Findings

Conversion request was logged after the statement cut; full amount billed per cycle rules while EMI took effect the following cycle.

## Resolution

Interest adjustment posted for the overlap period and billing corrected via Disputes Operations; EMI schedule regenerated.
