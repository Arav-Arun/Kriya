# CASE-0059

- Category: Fraud
- Priority: Medium
- Assigned Team: Fraud Operations
- Policy Reference: POL-002
- Resolution Time: 7 working days
- Resolved On: 2025-12-07

## Customer Complaint

Customer reports card misused after a phishing call; a transaction of ₹61,240 went through before they could block the card.

## Investigation Findings

Pattern matches card-testing: two sub-₹100 charges followed by high-value attempts at GLOBALTECH-STORE. Final attempt auto-declined by the fraud rule engine.

## Resolution

Fraud confirmed; network chargeback with fraud reason code recovered the amount. Customer refunded and enrolled in transaction-limit alerts.
