# CASE-0052

- Category: Fraud
- Priority: Medium
- Assigned Team: Fraud Operations
- Policy Reference: POL-002
- Resolution Time: 7 working days
- Resolved On: 2025-11-07

## Customer Complaint

Customer says a series of small charges followed by a large ₹61,385 debit appeared overnight from unknown merchants.

## Investigation Findings

Pattern matches card-testing: two sub-₹100 charges followed by high-value attempts at GLOBALTECH-STORE. Final attempt auto-declined by the fraud rule engine.

## Resolution

Customer-induced OTP sharing established for one transaction; partial liability applied per the board-approved matrix, remaining amount refunded. Card reissued.
