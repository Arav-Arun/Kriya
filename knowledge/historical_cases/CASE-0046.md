# CASE-0046

- Category: Card Declined
- Priority: Low
- Assigned Team: Card Operations
- Policy Reference: POL-003
- Resolution Time: 4 hours
- Resolved On: 2025-10-19

## Customer Complaint

Customer's card declined three times at a POS terminal; worried the card is blocked.

## Investigation Findings

Decline code INTERNATIONAL_USAGE_DISABLED; e-commerce international flag was off (default state).

## Resolution

Identity re-verified and PIN counter reset; customer completed a verification transaction successfully.
