# CASE-0097

- Category: Fraud
- Priority: High
- Assigned Team: Fraud Operations
- Policy Reference: POL-002
- Resolution Time: 7 working days
- Resolved On: 2026-05-21

## Customer Complaint

Customer reports card misused after a phishing call; a transaction of ₹3,531 went through before they could block the card.

## Investigation Findings

Pattern matches card-testing: two sub-₹100 charges followed by high-value attempts at QUICKPAY-GATEWAY. Final attempt auto-declined by the fraud rule engine.

## Resolution

Reported within 3 working days — zero liability applied. Shadow credit of ₹3,531 posted within 10 working days and made permanent; card reissued with new number and tokens reset.
