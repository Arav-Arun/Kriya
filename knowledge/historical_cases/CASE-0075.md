# CASE-0075

- Category: Duplicate Charge
- Priority: High
- Assigned Team: Disputes Operations
- Policy Reference: POL-001
- Resolution Time: 5 working days
- Resolved On: 2026-02-12

## Customer Complaint

Customer noticed two identical Flipkart debits of ₹4,269 minutes apart on the card statement and wants one reversed.

## Investigation Findings

Authorization logs show two settled transactions at Flipkart for ₹4,269 within 9 minutes with identical terminal and order metadata. No recurring-billing pattern on the account.

## Resolution

Merchant confirmed duplicate processing; duplicate amount of ₹4,269 reversed to the card. Customer notified and case closed as Duplicate Reversed.
