# CASE-0041

- Category: Fraud
- Priority: High
- Assigned Team: Fraud Operations
- Policy Reference: POL-002
- Resolution Time: 15 working days
- Resolved On: 2025-10-12

## Customer Complaint

Customer received OTP and transaction alerts at 03:45 AM for purchases they did not make, totalling ₹16,364.

## Investigation Findings

Pattern matches card-testing: two sub-₹100 charges followed by high-value attempts at PRIMEGIFT-CARDS. Final attempt auto-declined by the fraud rule engine.

## Resolution

Customer-induced OTP sharing established for one transaction; partial liability applied per the board-approved matrix, remaining amount refunded. Card reissued.
