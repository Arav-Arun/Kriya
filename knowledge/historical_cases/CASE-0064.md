# CASE-0064

- Category: Duplicate Charge
- Priority: High
- Assigned Team: Disputes Operations
- Policy Reference: POL-001
- Resolution Time: 4 working days
- Resolved On: 2025-12-23

## Customer Complaint

Customer disputes a second ₹63,207 charge from Myntra; merchant app shows only one confirmed order.

## Investigation Findings

Both transactions settled; gateway logs confirm a timeout-and-retry sequence. Merchant order system shows a single fulfilled order.

## Resolution

Merchant confirmed duplicate processing; duplicate amount of ₹63,207 reversed to the card. Customer notified and case closed as Duplicate Reversed.
