# CASE-0023

- Category: Fraud
- Priority: High
- Assigned Team: Fraud Operations
- Policy Reference: POL-002
- Resolution Time: 15 working days
- Resolved On: 2025-08-31

## Customer Complaint

Customer says a series of small charges followed by a large ₹24,667 debit appeared overnight from unknown merchants.

## Investigation Findings

Geolocation of disputed transactions inconsistent with customer location; merchant descriptors match a known fraud cluster under CPP review.

## Resolution

Fraud confirmed; network chargeback with fraud reason code recovered the amount. Customer refunded and enrolled in transaction-limit alerts.
