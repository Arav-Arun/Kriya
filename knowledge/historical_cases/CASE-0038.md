# CASE-0038

- Category: Card Declined
- Priority: Low
- Assigned Team: Card Operations
- Policy Reference: POL-003
- Resolution Time: 3 hours
- Resolved On: 2025-09-28

## Customer Complaint

Customer reports international website Amazon.com declining their card during checkout.

## Investigation Findings

Decline code INTERNATIONAL_USAGE_DISABLED; e-commerce international flag was off (default state).

## Resolution

International usage enabled with customer consent and channel limits set; retry succeeded.
