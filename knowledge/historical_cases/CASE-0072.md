# CASE-0072

- Category: Duplicate Charge
- Priority: Low
- Assigned Team: Disputes Operations
- Policy Reference: POL-001
- Resolution Time: 7 working days
- Resolved On: 2026-02-06

## Customer Complaint

Customer reports being charged twice by Uber for the same order of ₹82,975 placed on the same day.

## Investigation Findings

Transaction pair matched on amount, merchant, and auth code lineage; second auth was a gateway resubmission, not a separate purchase.

## Resolution

Merchant unresponsive within 5 working days; chargeback raised with duplicate-processing reason code and shadow credit posted. Credit made permanent after no representment.
