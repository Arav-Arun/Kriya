# CASE-0087

- Category: Fraud
- Priority: Medium
- Assigned Team: Fraud Operations
- Policy Reference: POL-002
- Resolution Time: 15 working days
- Resolved On: 2026-04-12

## Customer Complaint

Customer received OTP and transaction alerts at 02:10 AM for purchases they did not make, totalling ₹54,007.

## Investigation Findings

Card blocked on report. Device fingerprint and IP for the disputed transactions do not match the customer's known devices; 3-D Secure was bypassed via compromised OTP delivery. Vector classified as card-not-present fraud.

## Resolution

Customer-induced OTP sharing established for one transaction; partial liability applied per the board-approved matrix, remaining amount refunded. Card reissued.
