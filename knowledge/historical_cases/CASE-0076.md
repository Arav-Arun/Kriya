# CASE-0076

- Category: Duplicate Charge
- Priority: High
- Assigned Team: Disputes Operations
- Policy Reference: POL-001
- Resolution Time: 7 working days
- Resolved On: 2026-02-17

## Customer Complaint

Customer reports being charged twice by Flipkart for the same order of ₹8,935 placed on the same day.

## Investigation Findings

Authorization logs show two settled transactions at Flipkart for ₹8,935 within 8 minutes with identical terminal and order metadata. No recurring-billing pattern on the account.

## Resolution

Merchant confirmed duplicate processing; duplicate amount of ₹8,935 reversed to the card. Customer notified and case closed as Duplicate Reversed.
