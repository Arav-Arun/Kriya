# CASE-0032

- Category: Fraud
- Priority: Critical
- Assigned Team: Fraud Operations
- Policy Reference: POL-002
- Resolution Time: 7 working days
- Resolved On: 2025-09-16

## Customer Complaint

Customer reports an unauthorized online transaction of ₹72,431 at PRIMEGIFT-CARDS; card was in their possession.

## Investigation Findings

Pattern matches card-testing: two sub-₹100 charges followed by high-value attempts at PRIMEGIFT-CARDS. Final attempt auto-declined by the fraud rule engine.

## Resolution

Fraud confirmed; network chargeback with fraud reason code recovered the amount. Customer refunded and enrolled in transaction-limit alerts.
