# CASE-0031

- Category: Duplicate Charge
- Priority: Medium
- Assigned Team: Disputes Operations
- Policy Reference: POL-001
- Resolution Time: 2 working days
- Resolved On: 2025-09-12

## Customer Complaint

Customer disputes a second ₹66,494 charge from Reliance Digital; merchant app shows only one confirmed order.

## Investigation Findings

Authorization logs show two settled transactions at Reliance Digital for ₹66,494 within 17 minutes with identical terminal and order metadata. No recurring-billing pattern on the account.

## Resolution

Merchant unresponsive within 5 working days; chargeback raised with duplicate-processing reason code and shadow credit posted. Credit made permanent after no representment.
