# CASE-0054

- Category: Duplicate Charge
- Priority: Medium
- Assigned Team: Disputes Operations
- Policy Reference: POL-001
- Resolution Time: 5 working days
- Resolved On: 2025-11-12

## Customer Complaint

Customer reports being charged twice by Ola for the same order of ₹43,894 placed on the same day.

## Investigation Findings

Both transactions settled; gateway logs confirm a timeout-and-retry sequence. Merchant order system shows a single fulfilled order.

## Resolution

Merchant confirmed duplicate processing; duplicate amount of ₹43,894 reversed to the card. Customer notified and case closed as Duplicate Reversed.
