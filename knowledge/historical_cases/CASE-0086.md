# CASE-0086

- Category: Duplicate Charge
- Priority: Medium
- Assigned Team: Disputes Operations
- Policy Reference: POL-001
- Resolution Time: 5 working days
- Resolved On: 2026-04-01

## Customer Complaint

Customer says the Reliance Digital payment page timed out, they retried, and both attempts of ₹74,894 were charged.

## Investigation Findings

Both transactions settled; gateway logs confirm a timeout-and-retry sequence. Merchant order system shows a single fulfilled order.

## Resolution

Merchant unresponsive within 5 working days; chargeback raised with duplicate-processing reason code and shadow credit posted. Credit made permanent after no representment.
