- Category: Duplicate Charge
- Priority: High
- Assigned Team: Disputes Operations

## Customer Complaint

Customer reports being charged twice by Amazon for the same order of ₹21,401 placed on the same day.

## Investigation Findings

Transaction pair matched on amount, merchant, and auth code lineage; second auth was a gateway resubmission, not a separate purchase.

## Resolution

Duplicate reversed within SLA; customer advised on auto-reversal timelines for pending authorizations.
