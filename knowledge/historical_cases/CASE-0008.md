# CASE-0008

- Category: Card Declined
- Priority: Medium
- Assigned Team: Card Operations
- Policy Reference: POL-003
- Resolution Time: 2 working days
- Resolved On: 2025-07-07

## Customer Complaint

Customer's card was declined at Uber for ₹12,485 despite available limit.

## Investigation Findings

Decline code SUSPECTED_FRAUD_BLOCK placed by the rule engine after an unusual-merchant pattern; referred to Fraud Operations and cleared as false positive.

## Resolution

Explained the available-limit computation to the customer; payment succeeded after partial bill payment. Closed with decline category recorded.
