# CASE-0036

- Category: Fraud
- Priority: Critical
- Assigned Team: Fraud Operations
- Policy Reference: POL-002
- Resolution Time: 12 working days
- Resolved On: 2025-09-25

## Customer Complaint

Customer reports card misused after a phishing call; a transaction of ₹45,834 went through before they could block the card.

## Investigation Findings

Geolocation of disputed transactions inconsistent with customer location; merchant descriptors match a known fraud cluster under CPP review.

## Resolution

Customer-induced OTP sharing established for one transaction; partial liability applied per the board-approved matrix, remaining amount refunded. Card reissued.
