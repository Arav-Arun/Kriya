# CASE-0006

- Category: Fraud
- Priority: High
- Assigned Team: Fraud Operations
- Policy Reference: POL-002
- Resolution Time: 12 working days
- Resolved On: 2025-06-30

## Customer Complaint

Customer reports card misused after a phishing call; a transaction of ₹78,490 went through before they could block the card.

## Investigation Findings

Card blocked on report. Device fingerprint and IP for the disputed transactions do not match the customer's known devices; 3-D Secure was bypassed via compromised OTP delivery. Vector classified as card-not-present fraud.

## Resolution

Fraud confirmed; network chargeback with fraud reason code recovered the amount. Customer refunded and enrolled in transaction-limit alerts.
