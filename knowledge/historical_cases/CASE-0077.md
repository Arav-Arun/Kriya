# CASE-0077

- Category: Fraud
- Priority: High
- Assigned Team: Fraud Operations
- Policy Reference: POL-002
- Resolution Time: 7 working days
- Resolved On: 2026-02-20

## Customer Complaint

Customer reports card misused after a phishing call; a transaction of ₹35,918 went through before they could block the card.

## Investigation Findings

Card blocked on report. Device fingerprint and IP for the disputed transactions do not match the customer's known devices; 3-D Secure was bypassed via compromised OTP delivery. Vector classified as card-not-present fraud.

## Resolution

Reported within 3 working days — zero liability applied. Shadow credit of ₹35,918 posted within 10 working days and made permanent; card reissued with new number and tokens reset.
