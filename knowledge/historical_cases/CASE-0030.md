# CASE-0030

- Category: Rewards
- Priority: Low
- Assigned Team: Customer Service
- Policy Reference: POL-005
- Resolution Time: 5 working days
- Resolved On: 2025-09-09

## Customer Complaint

Customer expected 5x promotional points on dining spends but received the standard rate.

## Investigation Findings

Points expired on the 24-month FIFO schedule; expiry reminders were sent 60 and 30 days prior to the registered email.

## Resolution

Expiry upheld per policy; goodwill credit of 1,000 points applied once given long customer tenure.
