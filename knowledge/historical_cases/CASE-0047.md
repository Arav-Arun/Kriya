# CASE-0047

- Category: Duplicate Charge
- Priority: Medium
- Assigned Team: Disputes Operations
- Policy Reference: POL-001
- Resolution Time: 2 working days
- Resolved On: 2025-10-25

## Customer Complaint

Customer says the BigBasket payment page timed out, they retried, and both attempts of ₹1,551 were charged.

## Investigation Findings

Transaction pair matched on amount, merchant, and auth code lineage; second auth was a gateway resubmission, not a separate purchase.

## Resolution

Merchant confirmed duplicate processing; duplicate amount of ₹1,551 reversed to the card. Customer notified and case closed as Duplicate Reversed.
