# Team Playbook: Customer Service

- Playbook ID: PB-004
- Version: 1.5 | Effective: 01-Apr-2026

## Responsibilities

- First-line handling of product and billing queries: EMI conversions, reward points, statements, fees, and charges
- Triage incoming complaints and route specialist issues to the owning team with complete context
- Process EMI conversions and foreclosures with recorded consent
- Resolve reward accrual, redemption, and expiry complaints
- Handle fee reversal requests within delegated waiver limits

## Issue Categories

- EMI Conversion (POL-004)
- Rewards (POL-005)
- General queries: statements, due dates, fee explanations, app issues
- Intake and routing for all other categories to the owning team

## Priority Matrix

| Priority | Criteria | First Response | Target Resolution |
|----------|----------|----------------|-------------------|
| P1 — Critical | Mis-routed fraud/lost-card contact (route immediately), payment not reflecting on due date | Immediate | Same day |
| P2 — High | EMI conversion failed but full amount billed; redemption failure during checkout | 4 hours | 2 working days |
| P3 — Medium | Missing reward points, EMI setup requests, fee disputes | 24 hours | 7 working days |
| P4 — Low | Statement copies, general product information, expiry queries | 24 hours | 3 working days |

## Escalation Rules

1. Any mention of unauthorized transactions → Fraud Operations immediately; stay on the line until handoff completes.
2. EMI conversion failure that produced wrong billing → Disputes Operations for correction.
3. Reward adjustments > 50,000 points → supervisor approval.
4. Fee waiver beyond delegated limit (₹1,000 per case, one per customer per 12 months) → supervisor.
5. Suspected reward abuse patterns → Risk Operations.
6. Customer threatens regulatory complaint (RBI Ombudsman) → team lead + nodal officer desk same day.

## Resolution Guidelines

- Resolve at first contact where policy allows; a transferred customer should never need to re-explain the issue — write complete handoff notes.
- Quote EMI costs fully (interest, processing fee, foreclosure terms) before capturing consent; incomplete quotes are the top source of EMI disputes.
- For reward complaints, check the MCC exclusion list before promising credits — most "missing points" cases are excluded categories.
- Use plain language for fee and interest explanations; attach the exact statement lines involved.
- Log accurate category codes at triage — downstream teams and analytics depend on them.
